Meet The Houses

The Kiki House of Paragon is a dynamic house filled with many members who are genuine, loving, exciting, and a force on the floor! They began the very first Kiki house in the state of Arizona in 2021. Ever since then they have both put on events in Arizona and travelled to balls out of state. Currently the mother of Paragon is both Ellie Spinelli and Rylie Locker.

The Kiki House of Majestic is a powerhouse of heavy hitting members, although a small house in comparison to Paragon, each member has a category they conquer in. What they lack in members they make up for in talent when they hit the floor. A few words to describe this house would be: Grand, Ovahhh, hard working, and multi-talented.
